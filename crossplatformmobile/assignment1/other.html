What does cross platform mean in practice?

Works on android, iPhone or any other type of mobile phone or device as well as any desktop platform.

What's the difference between Responsive Web Design and the Mobile First approach

Basically it's the starting point of the platform/framework/library or your project.

In responsive web design you start working normally towards making a website for the desktop. As you build you implement frameworks or libraries that make your website readable on a mobile device as well. The point is, you start large and rescale towards small screens.

In the mobile first approach the starting point is geared towards mobile devices, you intend that the best experience of your app or website is on a mobile device. You then make it readable on larger devices. The point is, you start small and rescale towards large screens.
