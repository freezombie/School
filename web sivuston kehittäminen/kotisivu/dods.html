Day of Defeat: Source

This might aswell be called "Pelikahvila Nook" chapter, and maybe merged with CSS,but whatever

I played a hell of a lot of Day of Defeat Source when i was younger, and competed in every local event with some team

I made team Mech, competed with them at vectorama, didn't even make it to third place. We won every single event at the gaming cafe though

Iloiset Toverit Gaming Community which is actually just a group of friends who enjoy gaming together is also an indirect result of DoDs. We were making a group into Vectorama which had to be named. We named the group one year into "Mech ja Iloiset Toverit". The reason for that was it was Team Mech going to compete and we had some friends with us in the group so we could get a discount. Later on when we disbanded Mech we just started using the name Iloiset Toverit everywhere. We used to have an irc channel but nowadays everyone just uses skype.

So nothing special here i suppose, just felt like mentioning this game especially since i really had strong feelings for the game, and it also lit the competitive spirit in me for the first time.
