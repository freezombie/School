Short history

I was born. Some childhood things happended like learning to ride a bike and getting vaccinations. Nothing hugely interesting
I got my first computer as a christmas or birthday present in 1995, and since then i've been using the computer way too much, according to everyone else.
Well it wasn't all like that in the beginning, just innocently playing alley cat or stunts, then going out to hang with friends or play football.
I played football in Ajax, not the real one ofcourse, we won some Oulu cups or something. I was so little it is kinda hard to remember all the details, this was back around 1998

After football, i started doing general athletics. I was especially good at running and long jump if i remember correctly.
My mother signed me up for wrestling even though i was going to some tournament in Helsinki for the general athletics thing, i had zero practice for that for 6 months or so
I quit wrestling quite soon after starting it, after my first official match in fact. It didn't really seem like my thing, even though i did win the first match because my opponent thought he was doing WWE and broke the rules. He kind of won the match anyhow in my view, i was weaker.

At around this time my parents divorced, and I lived with my mother in a different house for some time. Shortly thereafter we moved to Spain.
In Spain my mother and his new upcoming husband bought a little restaurant near the docks. It was a fun time in general, though i mostly had "friends" who were in their retirement age.
I can see the effect of my stay in Spain quite easily in my life, i'm really missing some basic knowledge that was taught in Finnish pre-schools. Talking is sometimes a bit hard for me still, but i think that's also because of me spending so much time on the internet speaking english instead of finnish.
I played Football and did Taekwon-Do for the duration of my stay.

Around this time (2001 onwards) i really started to get into games, i had played them before ofcourse, but I started to think of them as a hobby, became a bit obsessed with them I guess you could say.
I really regret that i did not get into competitive gaming all that much then. I played mostly single player games, i really loved Deus Ex and Morrowind. Still do, planning to get tattoos at some point even. Make it so that those tattoos show the contrast between cyberpunk scifi and fantasy.

I moved back to Finland at some point, got into seventh grade. In Finland i finally started playing some competitive games like Counter-Strike: Source and Day of Defeat: Source. I had been a big fan of the earlier Day of Defeat too.
Then World of Warcraft happended. There went all competitive aspirations, but i really loved the game and it's community. I made many friends through and because of this game, even in real life.
