Counter-strike:Source

I didn't play 1.6 let's just put that out there and be done with that.

I used to run the server Oulu Finland Perkele in CSS. Also had a clan by the name of OFP. Not very creative i know but I was young and stupid i suppose.

We didn't take it very seriously, but we did have some matches and i think we won most of those.

At some point I started running custom maps and surf maps mostly on the server. Around half of the regulars became angry with this and left. I also left the stats tracking on even for surf maps so now I kinda realize i was an idiot. I however enjoyed all those maps, i was very tired with the normal maps at this point

Competed in this game aswell in gaming cafe tournaments. Usually some 20 year old dudes came and whipped our asses. Had fun anyhow

I have some nice memories of this game, i used to be good i think... Should had continued and made it bigger, but didn't because i'm not very stable/constant. My interests change very fast, especially if i don't have any IRL friends playing a game with me regularly

I remember in Spain owning everyone in gaming cafe's and being very smug about it. Young etc.
