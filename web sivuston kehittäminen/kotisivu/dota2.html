Dota 2

Dota 2 is my latest passion. I've played this game for around 2 years now, and have a played time of 2300~ hours. Before Dota 2, the only moba game i had played was a bit of Heroes of Newerth.

I don't really think i have any really notable achievements, but i did get second place in a lan in Kajaani with a team that had players that had only played very little.

I've had difficulty making a stable team that actually would like to compete and not "just play". I'm also starting to think it might be too late now

Nowadays i play mostly carry or offlane, but i used to play mostly support.

We have a skype call with the maximum amount of people allowed that like to discuss the game and ofcourse play it aswell

The pro scene is really inspiring to me, i love watching the tournaments and it has become a bit of a highlight of the year for me to watch "The International". A yearly tournament that was the first over a million dollar prizepooled tournament in history.

Last years internationals pricepool was 2,6 million dollars of which 1,6 million went to the winners: Alliance

The year before that had 1,6 million pricepool of which 1 million went to the winners: IG

Na'Vi won the first ever international and got one million dollars

I'm really lookking forward to this years international, i will most likely be supporting team DK from China, which has literally 5/5 awesome players.

I also made the jump, and I am actually going to an event in Frankfurt to watch it live. The event is called ESL Frankfurt and it will be held at a football stadium: Commerzbank Arena in Frankfurt. Really looking forward to this trip.
