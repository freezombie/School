Star Wars: The Old Republic

I really looked forward to this game. The reason i think it warrants a special mention isn't really the memories i had with this game or anything of the sort

I was a big fan of earlier games of this series. Knights of the Old Republic they were called.

I played a sith sorcerer, healer. I leveled very fast, and founded a guild with some friends for raiding.

Raiding felt more like fighting the bugs instead of fighting the bosses. This frustrated me so much i couldn't take the game seriously when some of the people were taking the game very seriously. We had cleared all content in hardcore if i remember right, there was no reward for doing nightmare mode, but the new leaders were planning to do that anyhow

Some officers were unhappy with me leading since i didn't see the point of being online 24/7 and not doing anything productive. Why should i be online if we are not doing anything?

I was online for raids, and i tried doing some of the flashpoints, i think there was some hardcore mode, but it was mostly ridiculous... You couldn't do them without raid gear which was ofcourse obtainable, but raids were scheduled activity. This resulted in me being offline for 4 days and online for 3, so we switched leadership. I wasn't a big fan of daily questing or doing pvp just for the heck of it. Even though Hutball was the best pvp i've seen in any game, there was no league system implemented yet. I got demoted into a veteran member at some point, felt like the guild was hijacked and left. Though i mostly felt tired of running raids since they were so buggy.

I would like to try this game again at some point if i could find people to do it with. Contact me if you would like to do this at some point.
